12×24 Jaw Crusher

10 tph portable jaw crusher

Role in Crushing Circuit: As a primary crusher, the jaw crusher handles run-of-mine material directly from blasting or excavation. It processes the largest feed sizes (up to 1,500mm) and reduces them to an output suitable for secondary crushing stages, typically ranging from 50-300mm depending on the application.

How It Works: Jaw crushers operate through compressive force between a fixed jaw and a moving jaw. Material enters the crushing chamber from the top and is progressively crushed as it moves downward between the converging jaw plates. The elliptical motion of the moving jaw creates a powerful crushing action while allowing crushed material to exit from the bottom.

Key Applications: Ideal for crushing hard and abrasive materials including basalt, granite, iron ore, reinforced concrete, mineral ores, and aggregates for construction. Essential equipment for mining operations, quarrying, recycling facilities, and industrial mineral processing.

Horizontal Shaft Impactor (HSI)

Heavy-Duty Impact Crushing for Recycling and Aggregate Production

Large Feed Acceptance (up to 1000mm)
High Reduction Ratios
Capacity up to 1300 TPH

Schematic Diagram

Operation View

Role in Processing Circuit: HSI crushers excel at demolition and aggregate recycling, processing concrete, asphalt, glass, and bricks. They serve aggregate, mining, and recycling industries, producing cubically-shaped road base and construction materials. Ideal for secondary or tertiary crushing of soft to medium-hard materials.

How It Works: Material enters from the top and encounters a horizontal rotor spinning at high speed. Blow bars mounted on the rotor strike the material and toss rocks against heavy-duty steel curtain liners. Multiple impacts between bars and curtains reduce material size while creating a cubical product shape with high reduction ratios.

Key Applications: Perfect for concrete and asphalt recycling operations, demolition material processing, aggregate production, and road base material. Handles feed sizes up to 40 inches (1,000mm) with capacities reaching 1300 TPH. Particularly effective for processing contaminated feeds and producing spec aggregate from recycled materials.
